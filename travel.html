1.Rangamati

Rangamati is a picturesque district located in the southeastern part of Bangladesh, within the Chittagong Hill Tracts region. Known for its breathtaking natural beauty, Rangamati is often referred to as the "Lake City of Bangladesh" due to its prominent feature, the Kaptai Lake. 2.Cox's Bazer

Cox's Bazer located in Bangladesh, is renowned for having the longest natural sea beach in the world. Stretching approximately 120 kilometers (75 miles), this beach is an uninterrupted sandy shore along the Bay of Bengal.
